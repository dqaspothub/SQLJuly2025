CASE Statement Tasks

Task 1 — Categorize Salary Levels

Question: Show each employee’s name, department, salary, and categorize salary as:

'Low' if salary < 50000

'Medium' if salary between 50000 and 70000

'High' if salary > 70000


Task 2 — Employees Earning Above Average Salary

Question: List employees whose salary is above the company’s average salary.


Task 3 — Highest Salary in Each Department

Question: Show employee name, department, and salary for those who earn the highest salary in their department.


Task 4 — IT Employees Earning More Than Finance Average

Question: Find IT department employees whose salary is greater than the average salary of Finance department.

Task 5 — Combine CASE & Subquery

Question: For employees who earn above the average salary, label them as 'Above Avg', otherwise 'Below Avg'.

Task 6 — Bonus Calculation Based on Performance

Question: Assume performance bonus rules:

If salary is in top 25% of employees → Bonus = 20% of salary

If salary is between average salary and top 25% → Bonus = 10% of salary

Otherwise → Bonus = 5% of salary


Task 8 — Department Ranking Based on Average Salary

Question: Display each department and rank it as:

'Top Paying' if department's average salary is highest

'Low Paying' if department's average salary is lowest

'Mid Paying' otherwise
